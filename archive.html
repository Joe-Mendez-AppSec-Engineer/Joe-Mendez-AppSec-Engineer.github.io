title of blog

July 21, 2023

Fueled by an unwavering mission to save the world from the catastrophic grips of Datageddon (and coffee), I served as a dedicated Data Engineer at the esteemed New York City Department of Education.

Leveraged the power of Databricks, Spark, Python, SQL, and Big Data technologies to architect and implement robust data solutions, safeguarding the future of education in the heart of the city that never sleeps.

Designed and developed scalable data pipelines, transforming raw educational data into actionable insights that propelled decision-making processes and empowered educators to shape the lives of over 1000000 students.

Unleashed my masterful prowess to forge and deploy impeccable data pipelines, a mighty shield against the encroaching tides of data chaos. Through tireless dedication, vanquished the specter of inaccuracy, resurrecting hope and propelling the world into an era of unprecedented data precision—a staggering 80% surge in accuracy, saving us all from the clutches of Datageddon.

Commandeered the helm to spearhead the revolutionary construction and deployment of a data lake, heralding a triumphant era of data accessibility and rescuing the world from the clutches of Datageddon with a resounding 50% surge in data accessibility.

Embraced challenges with unwavering determination, seeking opportunities to optimize data processing, storage, and retrieval, thus reducing latency and providing real-time insights for timely decision-making.
