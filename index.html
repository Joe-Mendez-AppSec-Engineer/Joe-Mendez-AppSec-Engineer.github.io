7+ Years of Professional Experience

Throughout the years, I have seen the tech landscape change dramatically. From companies of all sizes moving from on-prem to cloud, to more complex development cycles, and most recently, the rise of AI, I have seen how quickly the landscape can evolve, and how quickly businesses and Cybersecurity professionals need to adapt alongside it.

Securing Millions of Users

In my career, I have had the privilege to work on applications that have reached over 10 million users that spanned 26 countries, and encompassed user groups from age 5 to 70+.
